About me

Fredrick Sigalla is technology enthusiast from Tanzania, with the strong emphasis on cutting edge technologies. Fredrick is a full time tech entrepreneur and a seasonal employee. He is the founder of various initiatives, including Kasuku and "Rent Kwa Mwezi". As a seasonal employee, Fredrick has worked on both technical and management positions for various organizations including USAIDS Affiliate Jhpiego, Digital Opportunity Trust, Medical Education Partnership Initiative - KCMC and Techno Brain Ltd just to mention a few. Fredrick has also volunteered for Mozilla on MozFest 2017 as a wrangler as well as Mozilla Open Leadership Program 2017 as a mentor. Fredrick has paused for several events and hackathons both as a developer as well as the speaker, such events include Bits & Bytes Hackathon and DYE Summit respectively.
